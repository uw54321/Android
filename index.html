Our Android App's Privacy Policy

Introduction

This web page is intended to indicate the privacy policy of three Android apps: Ghost Leg Pro - Ladder Lottery, Unfair Wheel - Spin the Wheel, and Fair Wheel - Spin the Wheel, all available on Google Play.

Information Collection

We do not collect personal data, including location, financial, or contact information.

Log Data

In the case of an error in the app, we may collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This may include your device’s Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when using the service, the time and date of usage, and other statistics.

Cookies

This Service does not explicitly use cookies. However, third-party code and libraries used in the app may use cookies to collect information and improve their services. You can choose to accept or refuse cookies, and you will be notified when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse cookies, some portions of the Service may not work properly.

Links to Other Sites

This Service contains links to other websites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Please note that these external sites are not operated by us, and we strongly recommend reviewing their privacy policies. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age.

Contact
